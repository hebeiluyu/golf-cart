OEM & Export FAQ

Clear answers to common OEM questions, helping buyers make confident decisions.

What products do you support for OEM / customization? ⌄

We support OEM and configuration for EV golf carts, utility vehicles (UTV), and electric mini buses. Common changes include branding, seating, battery/charger options, accessories, and market-specific setups.

Can you support CKD / SKD style assembly? ⌄

Yes. We can discuss parts packaging, labeling, and assembly support requirements according to your target market and after-sales plan.

What is the typical lead time for export orders? ⌄

Lead time depends on configuration and quantity. For planning, we recommend confirming your required options, packing method, and shipping schedule during quotation.

How do you handle quality control and inspection? ⌄

QC is applied across production stages. We can also support inspection points and photo/video confirmation on key steps when needed for export orders.

Can you help with export packing and container loading? ⌄

Yes. We support export packing plans and container loading support, plus document coordination for shipment.
