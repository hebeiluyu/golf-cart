FAQ (Buyers Ask This)

Great for SEO and also improves inquiry conversion.

What is your MOQ for EV golf carts / UTV / mini buses?

Our standard MOQ is 1 unit for sample orders and small projects. For OEM / CKD or bulk orders, MOQ usually starts from 5–10 units depending on configuration. Please contact us via WhatsApp to confirm the best solution for your project.

Can you do OEM branding and custom colors?

Yes. We provide full OEM / CKD support including logo branding, color customization, seat layout, and accessories. Our team will confirm specifications and visual details before production. OEM branding is widely used by resorts, distributors, and project contractors.

What is the lead time and shipping process?

Sample orders usually take 10–15 working days. Mass production lead time is typically 20–35 days depending on quantity and customization. We support export-ready packing and container loading for global shipment.

Which battery options do you support?

We support multiple battery options including lead-acid and lithium batteries. Battery capacity, charger standard, and range can be customized based on your local market. Please contact us to confirm the best battery solution for your application.
